# Laundr-E

An autounomous laundry transport rover built for people who cant or simply wont carry their own clothes


Are you unable to carry your clothes due to a bad back, because you are elderly, or because you are just all around lazy? No matter. Laundr-E is our solution to your miscellaneous tasks! With our rover, you can store up to 30 kg worth of laundry without the fear of it getting harmed. This is due to the compact sensors kept on the side of the robot that allow for fast action, and with the omnidirectional wheel, it can move both laterally and longitudinally. 


# What is Laundr-E
Whether your maniging a bad back, navigating life as an older adult, or youve simply decieded that hauling laundry is beneath you Laundr-E was built for you.

Laundr-E is a compact Autonomous Rover desighned to handle the thankless job of hauling laundry around the house load it up start the mission and watch it go.
